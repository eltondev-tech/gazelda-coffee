Our Story

Every cup tells a story — this is ours

Chapter 01

The Dream Begins

Kapehan ni Gazelda started from a simple dream — to create a place where people can slow down, connect, and feel at home over a cup of coffee. It began with a vision of a cozy corner where laughter, conversations, and quiet moments can all exist together. From the very start, the café was never just about coffee — it was about creating memories.

Chapter 02

Why Coffee

Coffee was chosen because it brings people together in the simplest yet most meaningful way. Every cup carries warmth, comfort, and a reason to pause from the busy world. The goal of Kapehan ni Gazelda is to become more than just a café — it is a place where stories are shared, friendships begin, and moments become unforgettable.

"Coffee is not just a drink — it's a reason to pause, connect, and feel at home."

— Founder, Kapehan ni Gazelda
Chapter 03

From Dream to Reality

Turning the dream into reality took time, effort, and passion. Every detail was carefully designed — from the aroma of freshly brewed coffee to the cozy atmosphere that welcomes every guest. It was built with love and patience, slowly transforming a simple idea into a space that feels like home.

Chapter 04

The Grand Opening

The day the café opened its doors was a moment full of excitement and emotion. The first customers, the first smiles, and the first cups served marked the beginning of something special. It was no longer just a dream — it became alive through the people who stepped inside and made it their own.

Chapter 05

Moments Shared

Over time, Kapehan ni Gazelda became a place filled with stories. Students studying quietly, friends laughing over coffee, and families spending time together all became part of its heart. Each visit adds a new memory, making the café more meaningful with every day that passes.

Chapter 06

The Heart of Coffee

Every cup served is crafted with care and passion. Only quality beans and carefully perfected recipes are used to ensure every sip brings comfort and satisfaction. Coffee here is not just a drink — it is an experience that brings warmth and connection to every customer.

Chapter 07

Our Promise

Kapehan ni Gazelda promises to remain a place of warmth, comfort, and connection. Every guest is valued, every story matters, and every cup is made with care. The café will continue to grow, but its heart will always stay the same — a place where people feel at home.
